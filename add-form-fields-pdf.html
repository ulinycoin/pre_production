Frequently Asked Questions

Are my files uploaded to a server?

Never. All processing happens 100% locally in your browser. Your files stay on your device, ensuring total privacy and security.

Is there a limit on file size?

There are no artificial limits. The only constraint is your device's available memory. Most modern computers and phones can handle large PDF files without issues.
