Healthcare FAQ

Is LocalPDF truly HIPAA-compliant?

Yes. HIPAA requirements focus on the protection of PHI. Because LocalPDF is a client-side utility that never transmits, stores, or accesses your data, it effectively removes the 'Data Processor' risk, helping your institution maintain strict compliance.

Can I use it on hospital computers with strict firewalls?

Yes. Once the initial website loads, LocalPDF can function even if the connection is cut. It doesn't need to 'call home' to process your PDFs, making it ideal for high-security medical networks.

Does it work with legacy scanned records?

Absolutely. Our offline OCR tool uses WebAssembly to recognize text in scanned medical documents locally, allowing you to make old records searchable without cloud exposure.
