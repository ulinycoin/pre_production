About LocalPDF

LocalPDF is a privacy-first PDF toolkit designed for users who value security, speed, and simplicity. Unlike traditional online PDF tools that upload your documents to remote servers, LocalPDF processes everything locally in your browser.

Our Mission

We believe that working with PDFs shouldn't require sacrificing your privacy. Every day, millions of people upload sensitive documents‚Äîcontracts, invoices, medical records, tax forms‚Äîto cloud-based PDF services without knowing where their files go or who has access to them.

LocalPDF changes that.

By leveraging modern web technologies (WebAssembly, PDF.js, pdf-lib), we've built a complete PDF toolkit that runs entirely in your browser. Your files never leave your device. No uploads, no tracking, no data collection.

Why We Built This

Privacy by Design

We believe your documents belong to you. LocalPDF is built on the philosophy that processing should happen as close to the user as possible. Read our Definitive Guide to Private PDF Processing to learn more about our technology.

Privacy Matters

Your documents contain sensitive information. They shouldn't be uploaded to servers you don't control, scanned by algorithms you don't understand, or stored in databases you can't delete.

Speed Wins

Uploading large PDFs to servers is slow. Processing in your browser is instant‚Äîno network delays, no queues, no waiting. Just pure, native performance.

Simplicity First

Transparent pricing, local processing, and no hidden trackers. The way software should work.

How It Works

LocalPDF uses client-side JavaScript libraries to manipulate PDFs directly in your browser:

  • pdf-lib ‚Äî For merging, splitting, compressing, and protecting PDFs
  • PDF.js ‚Äî Mozilla's open-source PDF renderer for previews and OCR
  • Tesseract.js ‚Äî OCR engine that extracts text from scanned documents
  • React 19 ‚Äî Modern UI framework for smooth interactions

When you upload a file, it stays in your browser's memory. Processing happens using WebAssembly and JavaScript‚Äîno network requests, no server communication. When you download the result, the file is generated locally and saved to your device.

Open Source & Transparent

LocalPDF is built on open-source technologies. We believe in transparency and community-driven development. Our codebase uses battle-tested libraries trusted by millions of developers worldwide.

Privacy Sanctuary: LocalPDF processes everything in your browser. We value your privacy and keep our tools transparent and secure.

Our Principles

  • Privacy by Design: Your files never leave your device. Period.
  • No Tracking: No cookies, no analytics, no user profiling.
  • Security First: State-of-the-art encryption and local processing.
  • Performance: Instant processing with no upload delays.

GDPR Compliance

LocalPDF is inherently GDPR-compliant because we don't process, store, or transfer personal data:

Zero Data Collection

We don't collect, process, or store any personal data. No cookies, no tracking scripts, no analytics. Your files are processed entirely in your browser's memory and deleted when you close the tab.

No Data Controllers

Under GDPR, a "data controller" determines the purposes and means of processing personal data. Since LocalPDF never receives your data, we're not a data controller‚Äîthere's nothing to control.

Right to Erasure? Automatic.

GDPR grants you the "right to be forgotten." With LocalPDF, your data is automatically erased from browser memory the moment you close the tab. No manual deletion requests needed.

For EU Users: LocalPDF fully respects your rights under GDPR (Regulation EU 2016/679). Since all processing happens locally in your browser with zero server communication, your documents never cross borders, never enter databases, and never require consent forms.
